About Us

The Giving Hand Foundation, established in 2022, is an organisation that is committed to helping underprivileged people with By giving a hand we help others grow, we believe that we can make world a better place. in our communities. This organisation was created by students to help other underprivileged students in the Western Cape region but as our organisation continues to grow, we will be able to reach out a hand to not just students but to all struggling individual from all walks of life. Our Foundations belief is that No One Walks Alone, We All Need A Helping Hand. Although we’re all individuals with different paths, values and goals in life, at the end of the day we all had someone to help us and direct us along the way. The Giving Hand Foundation helps you be that guiding and giving hand in someone’s life. Everyone deserves an opportunity to a better life because we know that when people are given a chance to succeed they will often exceed expectations, that is why we’re dedicated to helping as many people as possible to achieve their goals.

Our vision is to help our peers with donations these may be stationary, money and clothing. We have created a website that will help people make donations. Our program is there to offer help to students in need and in future we aim to help as many people as possible as our organisation continues to grow. We have to bring up new generation of healthy, educated and inspired beings for our future. Our vision and mission are to help as many people as we can. As of now we’re helping our fellow peers at Cape Peninsula University of Technology with basic needs. By 2024 our organization will have expanded enough to be able to assist starving children in South Africa as research shows the amount of malnourished and starving children has increased exponentially during the pandemic. “Our kids are in a terrible state Malnutrition is slow violence and stunted children are unable to learn” - CRC founder Marcus Solomon.

Our vision is to reach as many people as possible globally and help as many people in world as possible. We are currently carrying our vision 2026 and 2024 missions, by the year 2024 we will be able to help all the starving children in South Africa. 2026 will be the year our organisation reaches all of Western Cape will be the year our organisation reaches nationwide so that we can help as many people as we can.
